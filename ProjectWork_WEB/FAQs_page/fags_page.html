Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What services does Caspian Global Auto provide?

We specialize in the safe and efficient transport of vehicles across borders, including luxury cars, family sedans, and vintage classics.

How can I book a car transport service?

You can book our services by contacting us through our website, phone, or email. Our friendly staff will guide you through the process and help you with all the details.

What safety measures are in place for transporting my vehicle?

Our state-of-the-art fleet is equipped with the latest technology to ensure the safety and security of your car during transit. We also follow strict safety protocols to protect your vehicle.

How much does it cost to transport a car?

The cost of transportation depends on various factors such as the type of vehicle, distance, and any specific requirements you may have. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How long does it take to transport a car?

The duration of transportation depends on the distance and route. We strive to deliver your vehicle as quickly and efficiently as possible. Our team will provide you with an estimated delivery time when you book our services.

Can I track my vehicle during transit?

Yes, we provide tracking options so you can monitor the status of your vehicle during transit. Our team will keep you informed at every step.
